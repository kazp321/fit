Progression rule

Hit the top of the rep range on all sets with clean form → add weight next time (+2.5 kg upper body, +5 kg lower body). Work to ~2 reps shy of failure.

Daily targets

~2,750 kcal · 130 g protein · 8–10k steps · 7–9 h sleep · 2.5–3 L water

Warm up first: 5 min easy bike/incline walk + 2 light sets of the first lift. Rest 2–3 min on big lifts, 60–90 s on the rest. Tap an exercise for its form demo. Starting weights are a first-session guess — adjust until the last 2 reps are hard but clean.

Sunday session —

Bike, brisk hike, or jog. Wear the chest strap. Stay conversational — if you can't speak full sentences, ease off. Load the Zone 2 workout on your watch and it will guide you (Training → Workouts).

Formula: 1 breakfast + 1 lunch + 1 dinner + 2 snacks ≈ 2,700–2,900 kcal. Mix and match freely. Each recipe is a single dish that keeps the house kitchen kosher (no meat + dairy cooked together) — but combine meals across the day however you like. If weekly-average weight hasn't risen ~0.5 kg after 4 weeks, add one more snack.

Breakfasts

Big Oatmeal Bowl700 · 35 g P
  • 80 g rolled oats
  • 300 ml whole milk
  • 170 g Greek yogurt
  • 100 g berries (frozen fine)
  • 1 tbsp honey · 1 tbsp chia
Simmer oats + milk 4–5 min. Off the heat, stir through yogurt. Top with berries, honey, chia. 8 minutes total.
Omelette + Avocado Toast680 · 36 g P
  • 3 eggs · 30 g grated cheese
  • Handful spinach + ½ chopped pepper
  • 2 slices sourdough/wholegrain (80 g)
  • ½ avocado · squeeze of lemon, salt
Sauté veg 2 min, add whisked eggs + cheese, fold when just set. Toast bread, smash avocado on top.

Lunches

Chicken Rice Bowl750 · 50 g P
  • 200 g chicken breast · 1 tbsp olive oil · paprika, garlic, salt
  • 95 g rice (dry) ≈ 280 g cooked
  • 200 g roast veg (pepper/zucchini/onion)
  • Sauce: 1 tbsp tahini + lemon + water
Batch tip: grill 3 breasts + roast a tray of veg + cook a pot of rice on Sunday → 3 lunches ready. Assemble and drizzle.
Tuna Salad Sandwich + Apple650 · 38 g P
  • 2 × 95 g tins tuna, drained
  • 1 tbsp mayo + 1 tsp mustard + diced pickle
  • 2 slices wholegrain bread
  • Side salad + 1 tbsp olive oil · 1 apple
Mix, assemble, done — 5 minutes. Swap tuna for tinned salmon or sardines whenever.

Dinners

Beef Noodle Stir-fry720 · 40 g P
  • 150 g beef strips
  • 90 g dry noodles (egg or rice)
  • 300 g stir-fry veg mix
  • 2 tbsp soy · garlic · ginger · 1 tbsp oil
Boil noodles. Sear beef hard 2 min, remove. Stir-fry veg 3 min, return beef, add noodles + soy/garlic/ginger, toss 1 min.
Salmon Traybake800 · 45 g P
  • 180 g salmon fillet
  • 400 g baby potatoes, halved
  • 200 g green beans
  • 1 tbsp olive oil · lemon · dill
200 °C: potatoes + oil in for 20 min, then add salmon + beans for 12–14 min more. One tray, no thinking.
Chicken Pasta Arrabbiata800 · 48 g P
  • 150 g chicken breast, diced
  • 100 g dry pasta
  • 200 g passata · garlic · chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil · basil
Boil pasta. Brown chicken in oil, add garlic/chilli 30 s, add passata, simmer 5 min, toss with pasta + a splash of pasta water.

Body weight

Weigh 2–3×/week, same time of day. Watch the weekly average, not single days. Target: +0.25–0.5 kg/month. Tap ✕ to remove a wrong entry.

⌚ Garmin sync

Import activities recorded on your Fenix 6. Strength workouts fill your lift log automatically (sets, reps, and any weights you entered on the watch); runs, rides and hikes go into the activity log below.

How to export from Garmin

Single activity (.fit — best for gym sessions): on your phone, open connect.garmin.com in the browser (request desktop site) → the activity → gear icon → Export Original. You get a .zip — tap it in Files to unzip, then import the .fit here.

Many activities at once (CSV): connect.garmin.com → Activities → Export CSV, then import it here. CSV fills the activity log only (no per-set data).

Note: weights you type on the watch during the workout are included; weights edited later in Garmin Connect are not part of the original file.
